Project Overview

Jaipur faces recurring water scarcity issues due to its semi-arid climate. This project focuses on creating a sustainable water management plan incorporating rainwater harvesting, water recycling, and optimized water usage.

Key Objectives

  • Mitigate the impact of water scarcity.
  • Promote sustainable water usage and conservation practices.
  • Incorporate innovative technologies such as rainwater harvesting and water recycling.
